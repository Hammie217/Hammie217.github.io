EEE105 Electronic Devices

This Webpage contains a series of computer based lectures that cover the detail of the EEE105 course. This Computer Aided Learning (CAL) material covers the same material as the normal lectures and is recommended to help you recap concepts that you find difficult and to use for revision purposes. You will find cross-references in the normal lecture notes/handouts to the CAL material that is relevant to each section.

With the exception of the “Revision of Semiconductors 1 to 6” file, which is a five minute MPEG film, all the lectures are provided as shockwave movies. To run these you may need to download a Shockwave player from the Macromedia site, but you should be prompted to do this automatically.

Each lecture is from 1 to 5 minutes long. Just click on the hypertext link to upload the lecture. You can stop, fast forward, rewind etc as you wish. Most of them include sound, so you may need use headphones if watching them in the IT centre.

If the material is difficult to read on your screen then click here for a suggestion on how to improve the visibility of the material.
