Reccomended books

The Art of Electronics

Horowitz and Hill

This is the best general purpose electronic engineering book I know of. Perfect for reading or as a lookup book. I've not yet read the x chapters but it's on my list.

Seeing the Big Picture

Kevin Cope

Very useful handbook size business book. Beautiful business examples using a bike shop as an example. A definite buy for those interested given the low cost.

Analog Filter Design

M.E. Van Valkenburg

Brilliantly written book for those interested in a deep understanding of analog filter design from zero to hero. Worth the cost to get the hardcover given the size. Again perfect for reading or as a lookup.
