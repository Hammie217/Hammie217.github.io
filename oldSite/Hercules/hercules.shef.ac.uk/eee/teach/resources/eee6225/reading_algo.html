The Advanced Encryption Standard (AES)
(formerly known as the "Rijndael" cipher)
1. Definitive description

The world-wide definitive description ot the AES algorithm is embodied in a specification published by the Government of the United States of America.

This document is referred to as Federal Information Processing Standard Number 197 (FIPS-197) FIPS-197(Definitive description of the AES)
This was based on a description of the AES by Brian Gladman Gladman02.pdf

An archive of the web-site from the development of the 'new' standard is still available at http://csrc.nist.gov/archive/aes/
