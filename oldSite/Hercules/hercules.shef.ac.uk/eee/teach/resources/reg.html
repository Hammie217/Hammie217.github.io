EEE Teaching Computers Registration

Most of the EEE Teaching computers can now be accessed by users with just a single user name and password. The computers concerned are to be found in rooms F3 and F5* of the George Porter Building, the Second year teaching lab, Griess lab and C1 MSc room in the Portobello Centre**

Once you have registered to use these computers, your user name and password will remain valid for as long as you are a student, so please keep your details in a safe place.

You will need your UCard to register, along with your CICS username, and you can register here.

Should you forget your username or password, it can be retrieved here.

If you have any further problems, email Fahmi Mohammad.

 

* Computers not included are the CICS managed machines
