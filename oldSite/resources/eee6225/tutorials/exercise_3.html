Tutorial 3 Exercise: GF(24) multiplier
Design the GF(24) multiplier using the Cascaded Bit Slice as shown in Figure 2 with m = 4. The only restriction is that your design must be synchronised to a clock. A table of all the elements in GF(24) generated by the irreducible polynomial F(x) = x4 + x + 1 is given below to aid you in your trouble shooting.
